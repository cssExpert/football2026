39 Days of Football

The road to the final

  1. Jun 11

    Opening Match

    Mexico kick off at a sold-out Estadio Azteca, Mexico City.

  2. Jun 11 – 27

    Group Stage

    Twelve groups of four. Seventy-two matches across all sixteen cities.

  3. Jun 28 – Jul 3

    Round of 32

    A brand-new knockout round. From here, every match is sudden death.

  4. Jul 4 – 11

    Round of 16 & Quarter-Finals

    Independence Day football, then eight become four.

  5. Jul 14 – 15

    Semi-Finals

    Dallas and Atlanta decide who plays for everything.

  6. Jul 19

    The Final

    MetLife Stadium, New York / New Jersey. One nation lifts the trophy.
